Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WaveKind FM.

How do I submit my music to WaveKind FM?
You can submit your music through our "Are You an Artist" portal. We accept high-quality WAV or MP3 files. Our curation team reviews all submissions weekly.
Is WaveKind FM free to listen to?
Yes! Our live broadcasts and standard podcast episodes are 100% free. We also offer a premium membership for ad-free listening and exclusive content.
How can I become a volunteer?
We are always looking for passionate individuals! Click on the "Volunteer" button in the "Join the Crew" section to fill out an application form.
Does the station host live events?
Absolutely! We regularly host live acoustic sessions, artist interviews, and local music festivals. Check out our Station Events section for upcoming dates.
