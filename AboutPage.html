THE SPARK

It started in 2018 with a dusty mixer and a single goal: to give a voice to the artists that corporate radio ignored. We believed the city had a sound that wasn't being heard.

THE GROWTH

From a local signal to a global network, we've expanded our reach to over 150 countries, proving that independent storytelling has no borders.

OUR MISSION

To provide a barrier-free platform for voices that challenge the status quo, fostering a global community through the universal language of sound.

OUR VISION

To become the world's most trusted independent audio network, where every listener finds a home and every creator finds an audience.
