How do I access the ad-free podcast vault?

Once you become a WaveKind Member, you gain instant access to our entire back-catalog of podcasts, completely ad-free.

To access the vault:

  1. Log into your account via the Dashboard link in the top menu.
  2. Navigate to the Vault tab on your user profile.
  3. Alternatively, if you use a third-party podcast app (like Apple Podcasts or Spotify), you can generate a private RSS feed link from your settings page to add to your app.

Can I submit my own music to be played?

Absolutely. We are a community-driven station and love discovering local and independent talent.

Please send an email to music@wavekind.fm with the following:

  • A brief bio of you or your band.
  • Links to streaming platforms (Spotify, SoundCloud, etc.).
  • High-quality audio files (.WAV or 320kbps .MP3) via Dropbox or Google Drive.
Due to high volume, it may take our curators up to 2 weeks to review submissions.

Why is my live stream buffering?

Buffering usually occurs due to network connectivity issues. Our stream broadcasts at a high-fidelity 320kbps rate.

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Switch your player quality from HD to Standard (128kbps) using the gear icon on the audio player.
  • Disconnect from a VPN if you are using one.
  • Clear your browser cache or try opening the station in an Incognito/Private window.

How do I cancel my membership?

We're sorry to see you go! You can cancel your membership at any time, and you will retain access until the end of your current billing cycle.

To cancel:

  • Go to Dashboard > User Panel.
  • Click on Billing & Subscriptions.
  • Click Cancel Subscription at the bottom of the page.
